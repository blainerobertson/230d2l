Buy Hosting Exercise

Introduction

Make sure before doing this exercise that you have already completed the installation of Brackets and XAMPP. Buying your domain name and site hosting will be the final piece in being able to produce web-based materials and make them available to the world.

It is strongly recommended that at this point you buy a generic domain name - your own name is suggested. Later, after you have gained experience and are ready to produce specific sites you can purchase more meaningful domain names.

Please ensure that wherever you purchase your hosting that the requirements below are met.

Hosting Requirements

You are responsible for acquiring a domain name and professional web hosting from a provider of your choice. Previous students have had success with these companies: JustHost.com, Hostmonster.com or Bluehost.com (each of these hosting companies provide a free domain name with purchased hosting).

  • Linux-based
  • PHP 5.5 or greater support
  • subdomains supported
  • live customer support provided
  • Unlimited bandwidth
  • Large storage (10Gb minimum)
  • hosting must be maintained for the entire duration of the semester

If in doubt that a host provider supports these criteria, contact the provider and ask. Do NOT assume that it does. All of the host providers mentioned in this document meet the requirements.

Go For It

The duration of your site hosting must last the full semester and the requirements for your hosting are listed at the end of this exercise, just in case you need to review them again. Go to your selected host provider’s web site and buy your hosting and domain name.

After buying your hosting and domain name, the provider should send you an email with all the important information you will need for your account. Keep the email and write down, in a secure location, the following information (you’ll need it soon):

Domain Name:           _________________________________________________

Account Username:   _________________________________________________

Account Password:   __________________________________

FTP Username:          __________________________________ (if different from account username)

FTP Password:          __________________________________ (if different from account password) 

During the purchase process you may be presented with add-ons that the provider wants to sell you. You really don’t need to buy anything other that your domain name and hosting. In addition, most providers will turn on the “Auto Renew” option. This means that when your domain name and/or hosting expire they will be automatically renewed and your payment method charged. If you don’t want this, be sure to find and turn this option off.

Registering Your Domain for the Class

After acquiring your domain name and hosting account you must register with the professor. To do this:

  1. Navigate to the Student Web Site module in the Table of Content.
  2. Carefully review the sign-up directions for adding your web site to the Web Site List.
  3. When done, go to the Student Site List page and add your name and create the link to your web site. Be sure to test the link to ensure it works!
  4. If the link doesn't work, troubleshoot and test until it does.

Submission

Your registration (including that of your domain name) with the professor is graded and is due by Wednesday of Week 2. Grading will be done from the student site list page (does your name appear and does the link work).
