To get a bit more depth, read this short document about Fitt's Law produced by Microsoft to see how it applies directly to content and controls on a web page.

How Do I Apply This?

In the Sketching a Wireframe activity you will be learning about producing a wireframe using sketching. As you do so think about Fitt's Law - Things are easier to hit when they are large and close, rather than small and far away. If you sketch a user interface with this in mind you will be enhancing the user's experience.
