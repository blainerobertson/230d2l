HTML5 Sectioning Flowchart

The good folks at html5doctor.com produced a nifty flowchart of common structural elements. You can download a copy using the link found on this page for yourself and use it to help you make decisions about what element is best for what you are trying to accomplish.

The Flowchart

Carefully review the HTML5 Sections Flowchart (PDF) to help you understand how to chose which element to use with the content.

Be Semantic

As you apply semantic elements to your web pages, it makes the pages easier for humans to read (as they look at your code) and easier for computer software (it knows where to look for the content it is searching for), for example all of the content of the page should be in the <main> element.
