SitePoint Premium

SitePoint Premium is a membership resource for textbooks, video-based web courses, screencasts and other materials that SitePoint has made arrangements to share via this service. SitePoint.com is an Australian company that produces many resources for beginner through expert web developers.

Join SitePoint Premium

BYU-Idaho has contracted with SitePoint Premium to make their service available to students enrolled in this and a few other clases for only $10 a semester (that is a substantial savings). We will use this service in our class instead of using a textbook.
