Module Overview: Personal Project

This last module of the course will not involve new content being introduced by the professor. However, it is possible that in the process of your work on your personal web site project that new concepts, techniques or processes will be learned by you in order to implement and complete your project.

Class time during this module will be for working on the project and collaborating with peers to solve problems or obtain help and feedback from peers, teaching assistants or the professor.

It is important that you meet the requirements as outlined by you in your approved project proposal as well as the technical requirement. Please remember the following timeline items:

Peer Review

The Peer Review of the finished project will be in-class on Friday of week 13. Failure to participate in the peer review process will result in a 50% penalty to the personal site project score!

Submission of the Project

The completed and polished project is due by 11:30 pm on the last official class day of the semester (Monday of week 14).

The finished project is your personal web site and will be accessed by visiting your web site using the student site list page.
