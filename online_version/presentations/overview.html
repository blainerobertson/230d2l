Teaching Presentations

What

During the course each student is expected to create a 7-minute presentation on a topic from the supplied topics list.

How

This presentation can be created using presentation software like PowerPoint or Keynote, or using a web-based program like Prezi, Haiku Deck, Google Slides, or something similar. After creating your presentation, you will need to use a screencasting program like Screencast-O-Matic or Google Hangouts to record yourself narrating the presentation.

Where

You will post your video to a semantic and valid web page in your own site. The page should have an appropriate heading with the title of the presentation topic. The presentation page should be linked to from your temporary home page. After being posted to your own web site, you will also post a link to the presentation in the discussion board for that week.

When

The presentation needs to be posted to your site and to the discussion board by Tuesday of the week your presentation is scheduled.

In addition to posting the link to the discussion board, Remember that you are responsible for monitoring the discussion board to answer questions and/or participate in discussions about your topic.

Review

Review the Teaching Presentation Grading Matrix and the Teaching Presentation Topics List. After reviewing these documents post which topic you want to present in the Teaching Presentation Sign-up spreadsheet.
