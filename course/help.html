Getting Help

For this class there are multiple avenues of help, but keep in mind that you must have tried to solve the problem yourself prior to seeking help from others.

Your Peers - it is expected and encouraged for you to work together to help one another. Meet face-to-face, call one another, use virtual software (Skype, Google Hangouts, etc...).

The Collaboration Boards - use the weekly collaboration boards to post and answer questions related to the topic of the week.

The Teaching Assistant - contact a teaching assistant to get help and answer your questions. The teaching assistants are shown below:

  • Naomi Bastion (campus, online) — bas15012@byui.edu
  • Joseph Canto (campus, online) — can16004@byui.edu
  • Asher Harris (campus, online) — har13013@byui.edu
  • Andrew Jester (campus, online) — jes15007@byui.edu
  • If you are an online student, contact the Teaching Assistant by email. The TA will contact you within 24 hours and the two of you can arrange a meeting time and means. Contact the TA early as it takes some time to make arrangements and actually meet.

The Professor - Check the Overview page for information of how to contact the professor.

CIT Open Lab is located in STC 347 (view the actual schedule). There you will find lab assistants who can help you if you are on campus.
