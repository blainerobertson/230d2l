Teaching Topic Presentation List

After reviewing the topics list below and the teaching presentation requirements, visit the Teaching Presentation Sign-up to choose your topic. Be sure to sign-up in the correct section. Do NOT remove someone else's name from a topic. Only 1 presenter, per topic, per section.

Your presentation is due Tuesday of the week for which it is scheduled. For example, if you choose one of the Week 3 topics you will need to have your presentation posted by Tuesday of Week 3.
